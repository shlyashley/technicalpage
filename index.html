What Is CHI?

CHI stands for: Cationic Hydration Interlink, which is a positively charged molecule that provides moisture and links into the hair fiber.The silk and protein bonds are Cationic (positively charged) to penetrate into the negative areas within the hair to build strength and resilience.

CHI is created under the Farouk Systems Group, that has the most innovative technology to hydrolyze the silk in liquid form or grind the Silk into a powder form.

CHI Transformation System

The Power of Ceramic Heat, Ions and Silk to Permanently Transform Hair

This scientific approach permanently transforms hair from frizzy, curly, wavy or even coarse straight hair into silky, beautiful, controlled, straighter hair by using the CHI Iron, silk and negative ions without the use of any harsh chemicals or ammonia.

Step 1: Analyze The Hair

Determine the hair type and condition of your client:

  • Virgin/ Resistant Hair
  • Color/ Chemically Treated Hair
  • Porous/ Fine Hair

Choose the correct Phase One Formula to be used. (Note: A Strand Test should be performed prior to service)

Step 2: Shampoo Hair

Lightly shampoo the hair with CHI Infra Shampoo.

Be careful not to stimulate or disturb the scalp during shampoo process.

Towel dry the hair.

Step 3: Prepare & Section Hair

Prep the hair by using the following method:

  • Apply Infra Treatment to the mid-strand to ends of client's hair.
  • Leave it on for 5 minutes, rinse, and towel dry hair.
  • Mist CHI Keratin Mist from roots to ends, and leave in.
  • Apply CHI Silk Infusion and work it in from roots to ends, leave in.
  • Dry the hair with CHI Pro Hair Dryer. This will ensure that the hair will have even porosity and help seals the cuticles.

Section the hair by using the following method:

  • Section hair into 4 quadrants: ear to ear, center forehead to the nape of the neck.
  • Isolate 1 inch of hair around the hairline.
Step 4: Application

Please follow the instructions carefully:

  • Measure desired amount of chosen Phase 1 CHI Transformation Solution in a plastic bowl.
  • Always start the application from bottom of your back quadrants (nape) followed by two front quadrants.
  • Remember to leave the hair line subsections until last.
  • Use 1/8 inch horizontal subsections.
  • Comb each section prior to application of Phase 1 CHI Transformation Solution.
  • Wearing gloves apply generously with tint brush to areas you want to transform (straighten) and keeping 1/8 inch away from the scalp at all times.
  • Do not apply against natural growth patterns, pay special attention to cowlicks and crown area.
  • Always follow the natural growth pattern of the hair, being careful not to add too much tension to root area.
  • After the application is completed, DO NOT comb the hair!

Retouch Application

  • Apply only to re-growth area 1/8 inch away from the scalp.
  • Avoid overlapping!
Step 5: Process & Testing

Always test in 3 different places (back, sides and front):

  1. Check the elasticity of the hair.
    • Take a small strand of hair between your fingers and gently pull in outward direction. You should feel the hair return back like an elastic band.
  2. Check formation.
    • By using a water spray bottle, remove product from strand and check the formation against palm of your hand.
    • Important! You are not looking for perfectly straight hair at this point.
    • When desired transformation has been achieved, hair is ready for intermediate rinsing.
    • Do not comb the hair!

Check the results every 3 minutes!

Timing is always approximate. But DO NOT exceed processing times! To accelerate processing time, low heat may be used with Formula A and B of the CHI Transformation Phase One Solution. Cool for 5 minutes if heat is used. DO NOT use heat with Formula C. Keep in mind the texture, condition and the porosity of the hair. Use your professional judgment! (Always strand test prior to service).

Step 6: Rinse & Dry Hair
  • Rinse with luke warm water for a minimum of 5 minutes.
  • Make sure all of the Phase 1 CHI Transformation Solution has been rinsed thoroughly from the hair and scalp.
  • Towel dry the hair.
  • Apply CHI Infra Treatment from roots to ends for 5 minutes, remembering to be gentle with the hair at this point.
  • Rinse and towel dry. Do Not Comb Hair.
Step 7: Flat Iron Hair

Hair must be completely dry prior to flat ironing client's hair.

Apply a generous amount of CHI Silk Infusion to client's hair.

Pressing roots and mid-shafts:

  • Start at the nape taking 1/8 inch horizontal sections
  • Begin by gently pressing with your CHI Iron in a downward motion from roots to mid-lengths 2 to 3 times stopping at mid-shaft.
  • Always use fine tooth comb to direct and control the hair.
  • At this point DO NOT take the CHI Iron through to the ends of the hair.

Pressing the ends:

  • Wait until you have pressed 2 to 3 subsections.
  • Combine these sections together, and continue to press the ends in exactly same manner.
  • Work on subsequent sections in the same manner until all the hair has been pressed with the CHI Iron.
Step 8: Bonding
  • Pour desired amount of Phase Two CHI Transformation Bonder into plastic bowl.
  • Apply the exact same way as you applied Phase One Transformation Solution onto the hair.
  • Process 5 minutes at room temperature!
  • Final rinse for a minimum of 5 minutes and towel dry client's hair.
  • Apply generous amount of CHI Infra Treatment and leave in for 5 minutes, rinse, and towel dry client's hair.
Step 9: Seal, Dry, Finish

For best results, use a round brush to blow dry client's hair for added volume

  • Apply generous amount of CHI Silk Infusion.
  • Start to dry and infuse with the CHI Pro Hair Dryer
  • Optional final seal with CHI Ceramic Hairstyling Iron and CHI Silk Infusion and style as desired.
